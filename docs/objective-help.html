What is an Objective?

An objective is a clearly defined, measurable goal that you want to achieve within a specific timeframe. Objectives provide direction and focus for your team or organization.

How to Create an Objective

  1. Navigate to the Objectives section - Click on "Objectives" in the main menu
  2. Click "Create New Objective" - Find the create button in the top-right corner
  3. Enter objective title - Write a clear, concise title (e.g., "Increase customer retention")
  4. Add description - Provide context and details about what you want to achieve
  5. Set due date - Choose a realistic deadline for completion
  6. Assign priority level - Select High, Medium, or Low priority
  7. Assign owner - Choose who is responsible for this objective
  8. Click "Save" - Your objective is now created!

Best Practices

  • Make objectives SMART - Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
  • Set realistic deadlines - Consider team capacity and dependencies
  • Align with strategic goals - Ensure objectives support broader company objectives
  • Keep them focused - One clear goal per objective
  • Review regularly - Check progress weekly or bi-weekly
üí° Pro Tip: Break large objectives into smaller key results to track progress more effectively!

Common Questions

Q: How many objectives should I have?
A: Focus on 3-5 objectives per quarter to maintain focus and quality.

Q: Can I modify an objective after creating it?
A: Yes! Use the "Modify" or "Edit" action to update any objective details.

Q: What's the difference between an objective and a key result?
A: An objective is the goal you want to achieve. Key results are measurable milestones that show progress toward that objective.
